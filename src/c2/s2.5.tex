        令
        \[
            A =
            \left(
                \begin{array}{cccc}
                    1 & 1 & \cdots & 1 \\
                    x_1 & x_2 & \cdots & x_n \\
                    \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots \\
                    x_1^{n-1} & x_2^{n-1} & \cdots & x_n^{n-1}
                \end{array}
            \right),
            B =
            \left(
                \begin{array}{cccc}
                    1 & x_1 & \cdots & x_1^{n-1} \\
                    1 & x_2 & \cdots & x_2^{n-1} \\
                    \vdots & \vdots &  & \vdots \\
                    1 & x_n & \cdots & x_n^{n-1}
                \end{array}
            \right).
        \]
        显然有 $S = AB$, 那么 $|S| = |AB| = |A||B|$, 注意到 $|A| = |A'| = |B|$, 因此
        \[
            |S| = |AB| = |A||B| = |A'||B| = |B|^2 = \left(\prod_{0 \leq i < j \leq n}(x_j-x_i)\right)^2.  
        \]
        显然 $|S| \geq 0$.
